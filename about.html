Welcome to Camp Apartments Razvršje

Located in the middle of the stunning peaks of the Durmitor National Park, Camping Apartments Razvršje invites you to fully immerse yourself in the natural charms of Montenegro. Spanning over 100,000 square meters, our camp offers a variety of accommodation options and exciting activities throughout the year.

Location:

We are located in the heart of the Durmitor National Park, surrounded by the unspoiled nature of hundred-year-old conifer forests. With marked trails leading to the Black Lakes, the town center and various mountain peaks, exploring the beauty of Durmitor is easy from our campsite.

Accommodation:
Camp:

Tent sites: Experience the magic of sleeping under the starry sky in spacious and well-maintained tent. Car camping: Enjoy the comfort of a caravan or park your own vehicles in safe and comfortable areas. Equipment rental: Don't worry about the equipment; we offer tents, sleeping bags, grills and more for carefree camping.Hygiene facilities: Our camp provides modern hygiene facilities, including bathrooms with hot water, toilets and more.

Apartments:

Our apartments offer a combination of modern design and warmth, ensuring a homely atmosphere during your stay. A variety of units: Choose from double, triple, quadruple and larger rooms, all equipped with kitchens, Wi-Fi, LCD TVs and more. Bungalows: Ideal for families and groups, our bungalows have multiple bedrooms, living areas and private terraces. Hostel: Traveling on a budget? Our hostel offers shared rooms with all necessary


Content and activities:

Adventure Activities: From hiking and biking to rafting and paragliding, we offer a wide range of exciting activities for every adventurer. Food Services: Enjoy homemade delicacies, local beverages and staples available for purchase at our campsite. Winter camping: Embrace the magic of Durmitor even in winter, with special facilities for winter camping.

Accessibility:

Transportation: We are located at a distance of 1.5 km from the bus station in Žabljak, which is convenient for travelers. Handicap Friendly: Our facilities are designed to accommodate people with disabilities and special needs.

At Camp Apartments Razvršje, we are dedicated to providing an unforgettable experience immersed in nature, with maximum respect for the environment and preservation of the natural beauty of Durmitor.
